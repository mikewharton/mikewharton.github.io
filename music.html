Musical experience

I have grade 8 and ARSM diploma (2019) on trombone, and many years of experience playing in multiple orchestras and ensembles. I have also given a few solo piano performances, notably winning the Symphonia concerto competition in 2024.

While at Bristol I was principal of both the University of Bristol Symphony, Chamber, and Symphonia orchestras. Some memorable works I have played include Shostakovich 5, Rach 2, Copland Appalachian spring, Martinu 3, Bruckner 9, Mozart’s Requiem. Below is a list of ensembles I have played regularly with:

Hampshire

  • 2024-2025 Winchester Symphony Orchestra (Principal)
  • 2012-2019 Hampshire County Youth Brass Band
  • Depping: City of Southampton Orchestra, Southampton Choral Society, Heritage Light Orchestra, Romsey Chamber Orchestra

University of Bristol

  • 2020-2024 Symphonia Symphony Orchestra (Principal)
  • 2022-2024 Symphony Orchestra (Principal)
  • 2022-2024 Chamber Orchestra (Principal)
  • 2021-2023 Big Band Society (Bass trombone)
  • 2021-2022 Symphonic Winds (Principal)
  • Depping: Symphonic Winds, Brass Band, Symphonia Philharmonic Orchestra

As well as this, I was the soloist and winner of the concerto competition for Bristol Symphonia playing Grieg’s piano concerto in April 2024. In May I also performed a lunchtime recital performing works for solo piano and accompanying Schubert's Arpeggione sonata.
